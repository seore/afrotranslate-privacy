Overview

GRIOT ("we," "our," or "the app") is an African voice translation application that helps users translate speech between African and international languages in real-time. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Key Points:

  • ✅ We do NOT collect personal information (name, email, phone)
  • ✅ We do NOT store your voice recordings
  • ✅ We do NOT track you across apps or websites
  • ✅ We do NOT sell your data
  • ✅ Subscription purchases are handled securly by Apple
  • ✅ We use RevenueCat subscription management
  • ✅ We do NOT require user accounts

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Voice Recordings (Temporary)

When you use GRIOT's translation features, your voice is:

  • Captured by your device's microphone
  • Converted to text using Apple Speech Recognition
  • Sent to Google Translate API for translation
  • Converted back to speech using Google Cloud Text-to-Speech
Important: Voice recordings are processed in real-time and are NOT stored by GRIOT. The recordings exist only temporarily during the translation process.

1.2 Translation Data

The text of your translations is temporarily sent to Google's servers for processing. We do not store your translation history on our servers.

1.3 Usage Data (Minimal)

We may collect minimal, anonymous usage data such as:

  • Which language pairs are being used
  • Number of translation requests
  • App crashes or errors (for debugging)

This data is not linked to you and cannot identify individual users.

1.4 What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ User accounts or login credentials
  • ❌ Contacts or photos
  • ❌ Credit card or payment information (handled by Apple)
  • ❌ Device identifiers for tracking
  • ❌ Browsing history

2. How We Use Your Information

Your voice and translation data is used solely for:

  1. Converting your speech to text
  2. Translating text between languages
  3. Converting translated text back to speech
  4. Providing the core translation service

All processing happens in real-time for immediate translation. We do not use your data for:

  • ❌ Marketing or advertising
  • ❌ Building user profiles
  • ❌ Selling to third parties
  • ❌ Tracking across apps

4. Data Retention

4.1 By GRIOT

We do not store any of your voice recordings or translation data. All processing is done in real-time.

4.2 By Third-Party Services

Google and Apple may retain data according to their respective privacy policies. Please refer to their policies linked above for specific retention periods.

4.3 On Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Offline translation packs (pre-loaded common phrases)
  • Conversation history (if Conversation Mode is used)
  • Premium subscription status (cached locally)
  • Transaction usage counter (for free tier limit)
  • Language preferences

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

4.4 Subscription Data

RevenueCat and Apple retain subscription related data:

  • Anonymous use ID (generated by RevenueCat)
  • Subscription status and purchase reciepts
  • Purchase history (for restoration and renewal)

This data is used solely for subscription management and cannot identify you personally.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • 🔒 All data transmission uses encrypted HTTPS connections
  • 🔒 No user accounts means no account security risks
  • 🔒 No persistent storage of sensitive data
  • 🔒 Minimal data collection reduces risk

6. Your Rights & Choices

6.1 Microphone Permission

You can grant or revoke microphone access at any time:

  1. Go to Settings → GRIOT → Microphone
  2. Toggle permission on or off

Note: Microphone access is required for voice translation to work.

6.2 Data Deletion

Since GRIOT does not collect or store personal data:

  • There is no personal data to access or download
  • There is no personal data to delete from our servers
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all local data from your device

6.3 Offline Mode

You can use offline translation packs for common phrases without any internet connection, meaning no data is sent to third-party services.

6.4 Premium Subscriptions

GRIOT offers premium features via in-app subscriptions:

  • Monthly: $4.99/month
  • Yearly: $39.99/year (save 33%)
  • Lifetime: $79.99 one-time payment

Managing Your Subscription:

  1. Go to Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions
  2. Select GRIOT
  3. Manage, cancel, or change your subscription

Cancellation Policy:

  • Cancel anytime before renewal to avoid charges
  • No refunds for partial subscription periods
  • Access to premium features until subscription expires
  • Subscriptions auto-renew unless canceled 24 hours before renewal

Restore Purchases:

If you reinstall GRIOT or use multiple devices:

  1. Open GRIOT
  2. Tap Premium or Upgrade
  3. Tap Restore Purchases
  4. Your subscription will be restored automatically
